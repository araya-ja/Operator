Python Operators Exercises

Bitwise Operators

  1. What is the result of
    a = 5 & 3
    print(a)

    • A) 5
    • B) 1
    • C) 3

    Answer: B) 1

  2. What is the result of
    a = 5 | 3
    print(a)

    • A) 1
    • B) 3
    • C) 7

    Answer: C) 7

  3. What is the result of
    a = 5 ^ 3
    print(a)

    • A) 8
    • B) 7
    • C) 6

    Answer: C) 6

  4. What is the result of
    a = ~5
    print(a)

    • A) -6
    • B) 5
    • C) -5

    Answer: A) -6

  5. What is the result of
    a = 5 << 1
    print(a)

    • A) 6
    • B) 5
    • C) 10

    Answer: C) 10

  6. What is the result of
    a = 5 >> 1
    print(a)

    • A) 2
    • B) 5
    • C) 10

    Answer: A) 2

  7. What is the result of
    a = 4 & 2
    print(a)

    • A) 4
    • B) 0
    • C) 2

    Answer: B) 0

  8. What is the result of
    a = 7 | 8
    print(a)

    • A) 7
    • B) -15
    • C) 15

    Answer: C) 15

  9. What is the result of
    a = 2 << 3
    print(a)

    • A) 6
    • B) 8
    • C) 16

    Answer: C) 16

  10. What is the result of
    a = ~(-2)
    print(a)

    • A) -1
    • B) -2
    • C) 1

    Answer: C) 1
