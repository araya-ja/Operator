Python Operators Exercises

Arithmetic Operators

  1. What is the result of print(5 + 3)

    • A) 6
    • B) 7
    • C) 8
    • D) 9

    Answer: C) 8

  2. What is the result of print(10 / 2)

    • A) 3
    • B) 5
    • C) 10
    • D) 20

    Answer: B) 5

  3. What is the result of print(7 - 4)

    • A) 1
    • B) 2
    • C) 3
    • D) 4

    Answer: C) 3

  4. What is the result of print(3 * 3)

    • A) 6
    • B) 7
    • C) 8
    • D) 9

    Answer: D) 9

  5. What is the result of print(10 % 3)

    • A) 0
    • B) 1
    • C) 2
    • D) 3

    Answer: B) 1

  6. What is the result of print(2 ** 3)

    • A) 8
    • B) 6
    • C) 9
    • D) 7

    Answer: A) 8

  7. What is the result of print(15 // 4)

    • A) 4.5
    • B) 3
    • C) 2
    • D) 5

    Answer: B) 3

  8. What is the result of print((8 + 2) * 3)

    • A) 14
    • B) 16
    • C) 30
    • D) 18

    Answer: C) 30

  9. What is the result of print(20 - 5 * 2)

    • A) 10
    • B) 10
    • C) 15
    • D) 5

    Answer: B) 10

  10. What is the result of print((20 - 5) * 2)

    • A) 30
    • B) 25
    • C) 20
    • D) 15

    Answer: A) 30
