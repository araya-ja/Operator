Python Operators Exercises

Precedence Operators

  1. What is the result of
    a = 5 + 2 * 3
    print(a)

    • A) 21
    • B) 15
    • C) 11

    Answer: C) 11

  2. What is the result of
    a = (5 + 2) * 3
    print(a)

    • A) 11
    • B) 21
    • C) 15

    Answer: B) 21

  3. What is the result of
    a = 10 / 2 + 3
    print(a)

    • A) 6.0
    • B) 5.0
    • C) 8.0

    Answer: C) 8.0

  4. What is the result of
    a = 10 // 3 * 2
    print(a)

    • A) 6
    • B) 5
    • C) 7

    Answer: A) 6

  5. What is the result of
    a = 2 ** 3 * 2
    print(a)

    • A) 18
    • B) 16
    • C) 8

    Answer: B) 16

  6. What is the result of
    a = 10 - 4 + 2
    print(a)

    • A) 4
    • B) 14
    • C) 8

    Answer: C) 8

  7. What is the result of
    a = 10 - (4 + 2)
    print(a)

    • A) 4
    • B) 14
    • C) 8

    Answer: A) 4

  8. What is the result of
    a = 5 + 4 % 3
    print(a)

    • A) 8
    • B) 3
    • C) 6

    Answer: C) 6

  9. What is the result of
    a = 5 * 2 / 4
    print(a)

    • A) 5
    • B) 2.5
    • C) 10

    Answer: B) 2.5

  10. What is the result of
    a = 10 * 2 + 3 ** 2
    print(a)

    • A) 29
    • B) 35
    • C) 49

    Answer: A) 29
